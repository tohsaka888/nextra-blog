# 议论文模板

## 开头段

- With regard to xxx(n. / 动名词短语 / the saying(谚语类)), viewpoints vary substantially.
- A tremendous number of people deems that ...
  - As far as i am concerned,i am in line with the view that ...

## 正文

- There are a tremendous number of factors/reasons.
- First and foremost, never can we disregard the innumerable merits/disadvantages that xxx(n. / 动名词短语) enjoy.
- To explain it further, the major contributing factor is that ...(题干的东西有什么好处/坏处)
- In addition, the second noteworthy reason is that ...
- （可选）This view is best illustrated by the case of ...(myself, elite, ..n.), (who / which) ...

## 总结

To sum up, from what we has been discussed abrove, it is natural to reach the conclusion that ...(改写主题句子)
